MEDiCUS


event
description

The event “MEDiCUS” AMiCUS Student Association, took place at the Adventist Educational Center in Stupini, Brasov on May 16 - 19, 2024. The Congress was for students and residents at medical schools in the country and each participant received a certificate accredited by the Romanian College of Physicians.

The following elements had to be created:

  • Concept

  • Social Media
    • Template pentru story-uri si postări
    • Numeroase postări pentru instagram

  • Print
    • Banner 2x3m
    • Stickers
    • Name badges for each participant
    • Diplomas
    • Certificates

  • Tehnic
    • Customized PowerPoints

logo

The central visual element is the AMiCUS logo combined with Caduceus (the serpent coiled on the staff of Hermes).

typeface

Montserrat exudes a modern and professional aesthetic that aligns perfectly with the MEDiCUS congress mission. Its clean design elements and geometric design elements evoke a sense of clarity and forward-thinking, which resonates well with medical students' commitment to education and progress.

Blue is a color that induces calm and tranquility, which is essential in a medical context. It also conveys trust and security, two fundamental values in the relationship between medical professionals and patients. Blue is widely accepted and does not generate strong reactions, making it ideal for a professional event where it is important that participants feel comfortable.
